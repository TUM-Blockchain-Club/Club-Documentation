# Setting Up Your Gmail Signature with AMBER

Welcome! This guide will walk you through setting up your official TUM Blockchain Club email signature using our internal tool, AMBER (Automated Mailbox Branding and Email Regulation). A standardized signature helps us maintain a professional and consistent brand image.

### Step 1: Visit AMBER

Begin by navigating to the AMBER web application using the link below.

[**Go to AMBER**](https://tum-blockchain-club.github.io/amber/)

> **Important:** Please ensure you are logged into your `@tum-blockchain.com` Google account before proceeding. The script is only accessible to club members.

### Step 2: Review Permissions

Google will ask you to authorize the script. Click on **Review Permissions** to continue.

<img src="/gmail-amber/Screenshot%202025-12-02%20at%2015.23.15.png" alt="Review Permissions Dialog" width="500" />

### Step 3: Select Your Account

From the list that appears, choose your `@tum-blockchain.com` Google account.

### Step 4: Grant Access to Your Account

AMBER requires permission to manage your email settings in order to update your signature. This is a standard procedure for this type of application. Click **Allow** to grant the necessary permissions.

<img src="/gmail-amber/Screenshot%202025-12-02%20at%2015.24.26.png" alt="Google Account Permissions Screen" width="900" />

### Step 5: Fill in Your Details

You will be redirected to the AMBER form. Please fill in your information accurately as this will be displayed in your signature.

- **Full Name:** Your full name as it appears on legal documents.
- **Job Description & Department:** Your role and department within the club (e.g., Member of IT and Development).
- **LinkedIn Profile URL:** A link to your LinkedIn profile.
- **Mobile Number:** Your mobile phone number, including the country prefix (e.g., +491234567890).

<img src="/gmail-amber/Screenshot%202025-12-02%20at%2015.25.57.png" alt="AMBER Information Form" width="500" />

### Step 6: Update and Verify Your New Signature

Once you've filled out the form, click the **Update Signature** button.

To confirm that your signature has been updated, open your [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/) and compose a new email. Your new, standardized signature should appear automatically!

<img src="/gmail-amber/Screenshot%202025-12-02%20at%2015.28.58.png" alt="New Signature in Gmail" width="500" />

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to the IT & Development department for assistance.
