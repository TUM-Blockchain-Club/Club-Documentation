# How to Set Up Vaultwarden

This guide will walk you through setting up your account for Vaultwarden, our club's password manager.

### Step 1: Go to the Vaultwarden Login Page

Click the link below to go to the Vaultwarden instance for the club.

[**Go to Vaultwarden**](https://vaultwarden.rbg.tum-blockchain.com/#/login)

### Step 2: Sign Up

Create a new account using your `@tum-blockchain.com` email address.

### Step 3: Request to be Added

After you have signed up, please contact your department head and let them know you are ready to be added to the organization. They will then grant you access to the shared passwords.
