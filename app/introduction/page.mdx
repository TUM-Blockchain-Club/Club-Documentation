# Welcome to the TUM Blockchain Club Member Documentation

This website serves as a central hub for all members of the TUM Blockchain Club. Here you will find everything you need to know about our club, from department information to important resources and tutorials.

## Our Mission

The TUM Blockchain Club is a student-led initiative at the Technical University of Munich dedicated to fostering a vibrant community around blockchain technology. We aim to educate, inspire, and connect students, researchers, and industry professionals to explore the potential of blockchain and decentralized technologies.

## How to Get Involved

We are always looking for passionate and dedicated members to join our team. Whether you are a blockchain expert or just starting, there are many ways to get involved:
- **Join a department**: Explore our different departments and find the one that best suits your skills and interests.
- **Attend our events**: We host regular workshops, guest lectures, and networking events.
- **Contribute to our projects**: We have several ongoing projects where you can apply your skills and learn from experienced members.

We are excited to have you as part of our community!
